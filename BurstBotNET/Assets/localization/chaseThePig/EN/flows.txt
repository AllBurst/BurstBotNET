The general game flow is as the following.

1. Each player will get 13 cards.
2. Exposure stage. Each player will have a chance to expose their special cards (providing they have one).
3. The gaming stage, each player has to play a card with the same suit as the first card of that turn, unless they don't have any card with the same suit anymore.
4. The final score will be calculated when the game concludes.
