**Rules**
- The rank of the **normal cards** the player played will be added to the current total.
- If all of player's normal cards will cause the current total to exceed 99 when played, and the player doesn't have any **special cards**, the player loses the game.
- The last player who still have cards to play will be the winner.

**Cards**：
*Normal cards*：
2, 3, 6

*Special cards*：
<:burst_spade2:930749903158792192> Spade A - Set the current total to 0 (zero).
<:burst_heart2:930749955914727474> Heart A, <:burst_diamond2:930749987044851712> Diamond A, <:burst_club2:930750022167957504> Club A - Switch all of player's cards with another player. Both players won't be able to draw an additional card after the exchange.
4 - Reverse the current turn direction, *i.e. P1 -> P2 -> P3 to P1 <- P2 <- P3*.
5 - Specify the player who will be playing next. The next player will be the one who plays next to the specified player, rather than the player who plays 5.
7 - Draw a card from another player. Both players won't be able to draw an additional card after the move, which means the player whose cards are drawn will lose a card forever.
8 - Draw 2 additional cards from the deck.
9 - Skip this turn and the next turn. Player won't be able to drawn an additional card after using this card.
10 - Add or deduct 10 from the current total.
J - Skip this turn.
Q - Add or deduct 20 from the current total.
K - Set the current total to 99 (max).
Joker - Switch all players' cards clockwise or counterclockwise, depending on the current direction (determined by 4).
